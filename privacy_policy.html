Privacy Policy - Nakshatra Aspire

Introduction

Welcome to the Nakshatra Aspire Society Management App. This app is designed exclusively for society members to manage maintenance payments, view history, and access important information. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information.

Information Collection & Usage

We only collect information necessary for society management purposes, such as member details, block/flat/shop information, and payment records. Your information will never be sold, shared, or misused. It is used strictly for reference within the society.

Data Security

All data is stored securely and used solely for administrative and member-related communication. We implement measures to protect your information from unauthorized access.

Third-Party Services

The app may use trusted third-party services (like Firebase) for authentication and secure payment processing. These services follow their own privacy policies in compliance with data protection laws.
